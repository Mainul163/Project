History Of Bangladesh

Our Country Bangladesh is my motherland. She came into being in 1971. Her land area is 1,47,570 square miles. About 16 crore people live here. There are sixty-four districts and eight divisions in my motherland. The main occupation of the people is agriculture. They grow jute, rice, sugarcane, potatoes and various kinds of vegetables in plenty. Our mother tongue is Bangla. We speak, write, sing and dance in Bangla. Her people are very simple and peace-loving. They are patriots too. They love independence. I am proud of being a citizen of a country like Bangladesh.

Beauty Of Bangladesh

Bangladesh has a natural beauty all over the country, Rivers are the most important geographical features in Bangladesh and also brings the natural attractiveness to it. Bangladesh has a plenty of places to enjoy and recreation,Cox's Bazar (120 kilometers) World Longest Natural Sea Beach is one of them.Kuakata is one of the rarest places which have the unique beauty of offering the full view of the rising and setting of crimson sun in the water of the Bay of Bengal in a calm environment. That perhaps makes Kuakata one of the world's unique beaches. The long and wide beach at Kuakata has a typical natural setting.
